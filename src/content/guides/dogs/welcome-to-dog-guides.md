Welcome to the dog care guides section of Pet-GoToPro. While our [dog product reviews](/reviews/dogs/) help you decide what to buy, our guides address the harder questions about how to actually live with a dog well — from puppy fundamentals to senior care, from behavior challenges to dietary decisions.

## What we are writing toward

Our initial guide queue includes several topics where we think clear, research-backed information is particularly lacking in the existing dog content landscape. Separation anxiety is first, because it is one of the most common serious behavior problems and one of the most misunderstood. The advice dog owners receive ranges from sound evidence-based protocols to damaging folk wisdom, and the stakes of getting it wrong include dogs being rehomed or euthanized over problems that were genuinely treatable. Senior dog mobility changes are another early priority, covering how to recognize early arthritis, adapt the home environment, and work with your vet on appropriate pain management. Nutrition guides covering kibble quality evaluation, fresh food feeding, and raw feeding debates come next.

## Training content with evidence-based methods

Our training content will lean heavily on modern force-free methods supported by behavior science, not because "positive reinforcement" is a marketing phrase but because the evidence genuinely supports these approaches as both more effective and less harmful than aversive alternatives. When we discuss training challenges, we will cite specific research and qualified trainers rather than relying on outdated wisdom that behavior science has since complicated or contradicted.

## Topic suggestions welcome

If you have a specific dog care question you have been trying unsuccessfully to find reliable information about, email contact@pet-gotopro.com with the topic. We pay particular attention to requests that come with context about what existing resources fell short, because that helps us write guides that actually fill the gaps rather than covering ground already well-covered elsewhere.
