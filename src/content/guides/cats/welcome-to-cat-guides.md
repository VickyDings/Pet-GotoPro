Welcome to the cat care guides section of Pet-GoToPro. Where our [cat product reviews](/reviews/cats/) focus on specific gear decisions, our guides section is where we dig into the behavior, nutrition, health, and daily-life topics that shape how well cats and their humans actually get along.

## What guides will cover

Our initial writing queue includes several evergreen topics that matter for most cat households. Indoor enrichment is first, because boredom is the root cause of a surprising number of behavior problems that get blamed on "personality" when they're actually environmental. Multi-cat household dynamics come next, covering introductions, territory management, and the resource-distribution patterns that keep peace when two or more cats share space. Feline nutrition is another early priority, including wet-versus-dry feeding debates, ingredient sourcing, and how to evaluate cat food labels without getting lost in marketing language.

## Health content with vet review

For any guide that touches on cat health, we work with veterinary professionals to review the content before publication. We'll always note in the byline when a veterinarian has reviewed an article, along with their credentials. Pet health content is one area where confident-sounding advice from unqualified sources causes real harm, and we'd rather take longer to publish than risk contributing to that problem.

## Suggest topics

The best care guide topics often come from specific questions cat parents have been trying to research without finding clear answers. If you've hit a wall searching for reliable information on some aspect of cat care, email contact@pet-gotopro.com with the topic. We prioritize topics with high search demand and low answer quality, which is exactly the gap reader suggestions help us identify.
