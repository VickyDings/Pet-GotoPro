## About the Pet-GoToPro editorial team

The Pet-GoToPro editorial team was formed in 2026 with a simple goal: produce pet product reviews that pet parents can actually trust. Our founder has spent years frustrated by the affiliate content that dominates search results for pet products — articles that are clearly written to hit a word count and funnel clicks toward whichever product pays the highest commission, with no evidence that anyone writing the review has ever actually used any of the products listed.

We started Pet-GoToPro to be the alternative. Our team approaches product testing the way an investigative magazine would approach a policy story: with research, with rigor, and with a willingness to conclude that sometimes the most popular product isn't the right one. We buy the products we review at retail. We test them in real homes with real animals. We document our methodology transparently so readers can judge whether our process justifies our conclusions.

## What the team brings

The people behind Pet-GoToPro have worked across the pet industry and adjacent fields. Between us we have hands-on experience with companion dogs across life stages, multiple-cat households, freshwater aquatic keeping, small-animal husbandry, and the occasional reptile. We rely on primary sources whenever possible: peer-reviewed veterinary journals, industry professional associations, manufacturer technical documentation, and direct conversations with veterinarians and experienced keepers in each species area we cover.

When we write about topics outside our direct experience, we say so. When we're uncertain, we say that too. The goal isn't to appear authoritative — it's to actually be helpful, which sometimes means acknowledging the limits of what we know.

## Getting in touch

Editorial feedback, product suggestions, corrections, and press inquiries should go to contact@pet-gotopro.com. We read every message and try to respond within two business days. If you've found an error in one of our articles, please be as specific as possible about where and what — we investigate every correction request promptly and post visible correction notes on articles we've had to revise.
