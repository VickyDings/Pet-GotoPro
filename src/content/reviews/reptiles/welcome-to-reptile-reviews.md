Welcome to the reptile reviews section of Pet-GoToPro. Reptile keeping is one of the most technically demanding areas of the pet hobby, and it's also one of the areas where bad advice causes the most preventable harm. We're approaching this section with the seriousness it deserves.

## Why reptile product reviews matter more than most

A dog can survive a mediocre bed. A cat can work around a subpar litter box. A reptile in an improperly heated enclosure, under the wrong UVB lighting, or on inappropriate substrate will develop metabolic bone disease, respiratory infections, or thermal burns — often before the keeper realizes anything is wrong. The equipment decisions reptile keepers make are not aesthetic preferences, they're husbandry decisions with direct health consequences.

This is why we think reptile reviews need to be more technically rigorous than almost any other pet product category. UVB bulbs should be tested with a Solarmeter rather than trusted based on manufacturer claims. Thermostats should be evaluated for hysteresis and probe accuracy, not just listed with specs. Enclosures should be assessed for temperature gradient performance under realistic conditions, not just measured for interior dimensions.

## What we're working on first

Our initial priority list covers the equipment categories that matter most for the most common captive species. Bearded dragon setups come first, including UVB bulb comparisons between Arcadia, Zoo Med, and Exo Terra, plus tank and enclosure recommendations for adult beardies. Leopard gecko husbandry guides follow, since leopard geckos are often recommended as "beginner" reptiles despite having specific thermal and humidity requirements that store-bought starter kits fail to meet. Ball python enclosures are next, with honest comparisons of PVC versus glass versus melamine options. Throughout, we'll be testing thermostats and temperature controllers from Herpstat, VE, and other manufacturers because reliable temperature control is the single most important piece of reptile equipment.
